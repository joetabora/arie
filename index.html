Our Mission

MKEinnerCityWeCare seeks strategic partners, sponsors, foundations, and community investors to launch Arie's Annual Ride for Autism Awareness & Advocacy.

🏍️

The Ride

An annual motorcycle ride and Autism Family Resource Fair that raises awareness of non-verbal autism and connects families with the resources they need.

💙

Our Vision

To create Wisconsin's leading community-driven autism awareness ride, giving a voice to children who communicate differently while building a network of education, advocacy, and support.

🤝

Community Impact

Educate first-time parents, connect families with resources, and advocate for stronger protections for vulnerable children across Milwaukee.

The Need

Families of non-verbal autistic children often face challenges accessing information, support services, advocacy resources, and community understanding. This initiative addresses those gaps by creating a visible annual event that brings together families, professionals, community leaders, and advocates.

📢

Increased Awareness

Raising community visibility for non-verbal autism and the families it affects.

📚

Parent Education

Providing first-time parents with the information and resources they need.

⚖️

Stronger Advocacy

Building momentum for protections and support systems for vulnerable children.

🌐

Community Partnerships

Connecting families, professionals, and community leaders in one place.

🔄

Long-Term Programming

Establishing an annual tradition that serves Milwaukee families for years to come.
